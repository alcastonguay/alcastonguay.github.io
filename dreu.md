I had the opportunity to spend the summer of 2015 working in the <a href="https://init.cise.ufl.edu/">
Intelligent Natural Interactions Technology (INIT) lab</a> at the University of Florida. I worked on
projects that explored natural interaction devices, such as the Microsoft Kinect and tabletop 
surfaces. Not only did I gain experience working on novel technology and SDKs, I learned about the 
importance of user research, and how to design and conduct usability research. Check out my 
[Summer Blog](https://www.cise.ufl.edu/~lcastong/DREU/) for more about my summer in Florida!

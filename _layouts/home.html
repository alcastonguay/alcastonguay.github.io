Hi!

Over the years, I've practiced many arts: music and design, as well as programming and technical writing. I've realized the connection between these studies is that they are all forms of communication. So I consider myself a communications professional, specializing in technical communications and programming platforms.

Please take some time to explore my work. If you like what you see, or are interested in programming as a form of communication, let me know!

xGraph at Introspective Systems

My most recent work has been at Introspective Systems, working on the xGraph platform. My work focused on usability and developer support. I worked with the team to develop code standards and API documentation. I collected this information and organized it on the xGraph documenation site using GitHub pages and jekyll.

Grateful Today

Grateful Today is a digital art project developed for my Mobile Applications class. Inspired by my Gratitude Board, Grateful Today provides a digital platform for your daily gratitudes. The project is still a prototype, but it includes most of the major functionality. I plan to continue development on this project, so keep an eye on it!

DREU Internship: Summer at University of Florida

I had the opportunity to spend the summer of 2015 working in the Intelligent Natural Interactions Technology (INIT) lab at the University of Florida. I worked on projects that explored natural interaction devices, such as the Microsoft Kinect and tabletop surfaces. Not only did I gain experience working on novel technology and SDKs, I learned about the importance of user research, and how to design and conduct usability research. Check out my Summer Blog for more about my summer in Florida!
